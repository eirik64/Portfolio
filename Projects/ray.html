Developing a Ray Tracer

About this Project

The goal of this application was to create a basic raytracer, a renderer that breaks away from limitations of using vertices with OpenGL to form triangles and approximate shapes that do not simulate how light travels through a scene. It also makes it difficult to achieve photo realistic results. This raytracer is meant to support reflections, texture mapping, shadows, more advanced lighting.

One day I wish to show my product on my platform but the difficulty of embedding the GUI into a website such as this is beyond my current capabilities. Furthermore, as I am still a student at Brown university I would not want to be breaking any collaboration policies by publicizing my work and robbing future students the joy and hard work meant to be put into this application.
